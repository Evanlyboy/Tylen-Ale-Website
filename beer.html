Our Beer

We try our best to offer a variety of beers for all our beer loving fans. As of this month we have added 2 new beers into the Tylen-Ale family. Our newest beers are out new West Coast IPA, along with our new Tylen-Ale Stout for all those dark beer enthusiasts. If you would rather buy in store, our bottle shop is always open!

Original Lager

Nothing beats the crisp feel of a Tylen-Ale original Lager. This bad boy hits the spot for any occasion and make all your pain go away.

Red Tylen-Ale

The red ale is a delicious beer. It is a hoppy, buttery beer that is a much dryer beer than the original, however it still captures that amazing Tylen-Ale taste we all love.

West Coast IPA

Brewed using only the finest hops, this pale ale is sure to leave your taste buds partying all night. This herbal and citrus ale is a must have for any and all beer lovers.

Tylen-Ale Stout

The New Tylen-Ale Stout is sure to put some hair on your chest. This Dark Beer has a bitter taste but goes down as smooth as water
