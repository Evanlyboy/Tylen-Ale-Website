Welcome

Hello there, and welcome to the official Tylen-Ale beer website. We here at Tylen-Ale would like to thank you for taking the time out of your day to explore our site. If you want to read about Tylen-Ale you can go to our About Us page, or if your just looking to buy a couple crisp & clean beers, our beer page has you covered. For any questions or concerns you have, feel free to fill out our contact form and a representative would love to help you out. So go on, and thanks again for choosing Tylen-Ale.

Location

Are you more of a hands on person? Are you too drunk to figure out our website? Well then come one down to the Tylen-Ale Craft Beer Emporium located at 1680 Halsey Avenue, Toronto, ON, M3B 2W6. We have a restaurant that serves amazing food along with all our beers, and there is an outdoor patio with games for the kids. So come enjoy some beers and hangout with the family.
